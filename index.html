Welcome to Chef Sebastiani website!

When I was a child my mother inspired me being very creative with everything she cooked. With patience, she taught me how to cook amazing meals using very inexpensive ingredients. My mother loved to cook and prepare large meals for the family, the neighborhood, and really anyone else who stopped by her home. Watching her express her passion for good food, I first began to develop a love for cooking. Having the unique privilege of being a part of a family with both Latino-American and Italian roots, growing up with both mixed cultures and I also had the opportunity to learn cooking skills in theworld renowned Le Cordon Bleu, in Paris France. Living in different countries around the world, Peru, France, Switzerland, Italy, Germany, Japan and United States. These rich culinary experiences as a child propelled me toward a career in cooking. Over the last few years, I have had the opportunity to work in many different areas including: French cuisine (Brio Restaurant in San Mateo, CA), Italian cuisine (Spiedo Restaurant in San Mateo, CA), Mediterranean cuisine (Oberon Restaurant, San Francisco), Japanese delights (Mercury Restaurant, San Francisco, CA), American Continental cuisine (Lake Merced Golf and Country Club, San Francisco, CA), Frenchdesserts (Arthur Park Restaurant in San Francisco, CA). On the other hand, I also got extensive training in Japan (Tokyo, Tokirawaand Ikebukuro) learning and practicing Japanese cuisine and the Ikebana art. As a personal chef, I have extensive training in food handling, health and nutrition. I have experience preparing food for people with specific dietary needs relating to high blood pressure, weight loss, and lowering cholesterol. Hopefully this brief biography has piqued your interest in my services here at Chancay Traditional Peruvian Restaurant. I am grateful for the opportunity to share my background with you.
